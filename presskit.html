Description

Growing up is not easy for a demon kid. Fara & The Eye of Darkness is a dimension-hopping adventure with rogue-like elements. Play as Fara, a Demon Witch who is fighting for her family as she cleanses her galaxy of a mysterious corruption. Interact with the inhabitants of strange, procedurally-generated worlds and battle exotic creatures using card-based spell attacks. Grow your spell card collection and customize your own play-style by building countless decks to help Fara on her magical quest.

History

Spaceboy Games is an independent studio that stems from the world of Twitter crushes. Once upon a time, on the internet, a sightly pixel lord, Sandy Gordon, caught the eye of a scrubby programmer with the Earth-name, Zack Bell. Their love was mutual. After two failed attempts at children, they adopted their beautifully foxy counterpart, Fellipe Martins. The three of them reside at completely arbitrary locations where they try not to starve while making stuff.
